CS 677 Data Visualization Final Project

IMDb Data Visualization
Bhadhan Roy Joy (joyroy@uab.edu)

Target of the project work

  • Explore d3.js functionalities
  • Explore IMDb dataset
  • Most popular movies by genre and year
  • Genre popularity (number of movies of a genre) change over years
  • Which age is the most productive for directors overall?
  • At what age directors produced their highest rated work in general?
  • Director's movies vs age with rating and number of votes.
  • Connectivity graph between directors and actors

Dataset:

https://www.imdb.com/interfaces/

https://datasets.imdbws.com/

Preprocessing Data

Data cleaned and prepared for d3.js with Pandas

Only included movies having at least 1000 votes
